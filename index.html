Pandat

Pandoc For Data

What Is Pandat?

Pandat is data converter that can convert both to and from a multitude of data types! We save you from having to rewrite your data if you all of the sudden need it in a different format. Pandat makes data conversion as simple as possible! We offer both a live web based platform and an npm package.

What Conversions Does Pandat Offer?

Pandat offers any combination of SQL, JSON, XML, and CSV conversion. In addition each of the formats can be outputted to Graphviz.

How Does Pandat Work?

Pandat works by converting any data given to it to our original text format. We have designed this text format to be easily convertable both to and from any of our currently working text formats. You don't have to worry about this though! As Pandat does all of the converting work for you!
